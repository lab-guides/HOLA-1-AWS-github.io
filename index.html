The goal of this Lab

In this quick tutorial, you will learn how to:

 

  • Create an Amazon Web Services (AWS) account; the single sign-in identity or AWS account root user that has complete access to all AWS services and resources in the account. 
  • Explore AWS Product offerings and pricing tiers. 
  • Create an AWS Budgets with Thresholds
  • Analyze costs associated with your usage.

The outcome of this Lab

At the completion of this Lab, you will be able to start working on your AWS labs within the free tier offerings.

If your free tier is already expired you will find the estimated costs per lab in the cover sheet.

Our aim is to remain within the free offerings or keep costs below $1 for all labs.

 

We hope you will have a pleasant learning journey.
