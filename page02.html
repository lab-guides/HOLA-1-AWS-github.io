Sign-up with AWS (or Sign-in)

 

Goal

:

Create an AWS account as “root” user and explore the AWS Management Console.

Outcome

:

At the completion of this section you will be able to access all AWS Products and Services for the creation of Labs and BluePrints.

 

IMPORTANT

To create your AWS account you will need to provide a username and your personal details.

A personal credit card is required for approval.

Sign-up

  1. Navigate in your browser to the AWS Account sign-up page

Complete the Sign-up wizard to create your account:

Enter Email & Password, Account name and click “Continue”

 

  

Screenshot 12 – Email, Password, Account name


 

 

  1. Complete your contact information and click “Create Account and Continue”

Screenshot 13 – Contact information


 

 

  1. Select the Basic Plan.

  1. Complete your payment information and final steps to create your Account:
