Explore the AWS Free Tier

 

Goal

:

In this section you will explore the different pricing tiers of the AWS product categories and perform a pricing estimate.

Outcome

:

At the completion of this section you will be able to estimate costs across the AWS products and services.

 

This lab is based on the online AWS getting started guide:

https://aws.amazon.com/getting-started/hands-on/control-your-costs-free-tier-budgets/

 

Access the Free Tier page

  1. Browse to the AWS Free tier Page 

Screenshot 1 – AWS Free Tier home page


Explore 12 month free offers

  1. Scroll down below to the “Free Tier Details.

 

  1. Under the “Tier Type” filter, tick the following checkboxes

Filter (check):

Tier Type

: 12 month free

Product Categories

: Compute

 

  1. You should see that you have 750 hours of free compute per month of Linux, RHEL, or SLES t2.micro or t3.micro instance dependent on region.

 

 

Screenshot 2 – AWS Free tier- EC2 and ELB

 

  1. Navigate to the Amazon Marketplace to look at a Free Tier application called “LAMP”.

 

Screenshot 3 – LAMP in Market Place is part of Free Tier

 

  1. Read the description in the Total Price window.

Many vendors offer products through Amazon market place that are part of the Free Tier. However the product will be based upon vendor recommended sizing of your virtual server (Instance type), in this case the instance type “t3a.small”.

 

These recommended sizing’s in general fall outside the free tier; you will have to explicitly select a t2.micro or t3.micro instance during the launch!
